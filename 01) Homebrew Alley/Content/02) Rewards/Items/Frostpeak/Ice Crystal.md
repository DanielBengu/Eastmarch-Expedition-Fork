#### Ice Crystal (X GP)
*Item, Gem*

A Crystal mined from the inside of the Frostpeak Mountain, this crystal can be used during the owner's [[Downtime Activities|downtime activity]] to do one of the following (crystal will be consumed in the process):
- Upgrade a weapon, giving a permanent +1 Cold DMG.
- Create a [[Potion of Cold Resistance]].
- Create a [[Potion of Cold Mastery]] (advantage on spells that deal Cold damage for 1 hour).
